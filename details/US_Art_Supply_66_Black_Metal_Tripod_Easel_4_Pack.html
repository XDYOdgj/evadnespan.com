U.S. Art Supply's 66-inch Black Metal Tripod Easels

Whether you're an artist, designer, or event planner, these 4-pack tripod easels are built to meet your creative needs. Designed for maximum flexibility, they offer adjustable height from 20 to 55 inches, making them ideal for both indoor and outdoor use. The sturdy black metal frame ensures strength and stability, even when supporting large art pieces like 32-inch canvas boards.

Each easel is equipped with a one-touch flip-lock mechanism, allowing you to change height in seconds and set up quickly. The included portable bag makes transport and storage a breeze, while the compact fold design collapses to just 18 inches. This makes the easels perfect for traveling, exhibitions, or professional projects.

Featuring non-skid rubber feet, these easels protect surfaces and provide a secure base. The adjustable horizontal fixing tray with vertical shelf brackets offers a flexible and safe setup for different art formats. Whether you're painting on-site, displaying artwork in a gallery, or setting up a presentation in an office, these easels combine quality, durability, and convenience in one essential tool.
